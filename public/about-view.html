We specialize in high-end new construction, additions, remodels and can provide a team of design professionals. Owner, Eric Whiting, appreciates long-standing working relationships with our clients and takes pride in the fact that our business has been built upon client referrals. Over the years, we have worked with local professionals who have become part of the Overbrook team. We strive to make the often times overwhelming process of building or remodeling an enjoyable and satisfying partnership for everyone involved.
